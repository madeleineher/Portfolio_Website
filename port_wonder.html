A hackathon project for "& The Wonders"

A 42 Paris X IFM Design and Fashion Hackathon

This was a collaborative project executed between two fashion design students from IFM, specializing in accessories, and two 42 Paris students, at the beginning of their courses, at the time.


During the course of this project, I was responsible with executing the desired look of the e-commerce website the IFM students had envisaged. Our collaborative ideas came to life after several meetings beween us, 42 students, and the IFM students.

We collectively brainstormed on how to tell the IFM students' story of unique bags resembling rocks and stones. We looked at other simple websites for our ideas and exchanged constantly with our fashion counterparts for advice and approval.

We designed a mockup with Adobe's XD tool and slowly realized how the website's design would look across the different pages that the website required. Once our mock-up was approved, me and my 42 peer created the website using HTML and CSS.

At the completion of the website, we regrouped to prepare our presentation for a board that would decide if we were worthy of going onto the second stage before incubation. However, despite not having made it to the second round, we recieved a lot of praise and positive feedback on our product because of its niche aspect and website because of its simple yet efficient design.
