My first "Angular App"

A simple game of Tic-Tac-Toe

An experimental project done in Angular, you can play with the app here. During this project, I gained experience with Angular life-cycles methods (ngOnInit), I also used a typescript getter, and implemented structural directives (ngIf and ngFor).


The app is hosted with Firebase, and uses Nebular for styling, I chose the cosmic theme.
